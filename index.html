PROJECT

COMPUTERIZED GRADING SYSTEM

The Computerized Grading System is designed to ease the workload of the educators in the institution. By simply imputing the test scores, the performance task scores, the system will automatically compute the final grades and the average of the students accurately and efficiently.

The Computerized Grading System represents a significant leap forward in educational technology, specifically tailored to alleviate the administrative burdens faced by educators in today's academic institutions. This innovative system is engineered to streamline the grading process, ensuring that educators can focus more on teaching and less on the tedious task of manual grade calculations.

By integrating advanced algorithms and user-friendly interfaces, the system allows teachers to input test scores and performance task scores with ease. Once the data is entered, the system's sophisticated software takes over, automatically computing each student's final grades and class averages with precision and efficiency.

This not only saves valuable time but also reduces the likelihood of human error, thereby enhancing the accuracy and reliability of the grading process. The Computerized Grading System stands as a testament to the power of technology in transforming traditional educational practices, fostering a more productive and equitable learning environment for both educators and students alike.
